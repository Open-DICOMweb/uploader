The American College of Radiology

The Harvey L. Neiman Health Policy Institute (HPI) will study the value and role of radiology in evolving health care delivery and payment systems, including quality based approaches to care and the impact of medical imaging on overall health care costs. HPI research will provide a foundation for evidence-based imaging policy to improve patient care and bolster efficient, effective use of health care resources.

For more information, please visit the Neiman Institute website at www.neimanhpi.org .
